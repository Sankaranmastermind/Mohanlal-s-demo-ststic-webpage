Thudarum - 2025

  • 25 April 2025
  • 24

Mohanlal's latest film, Thudarum, has surpassed his own blockbuster Pulimurugan to become the sixth highest-grossing Malayalam film ever, amassing ₹79.85 crore. Directed by Tharun Moorthy, the emotional family drama resonated with audiences in Kerala and overseas. Thudarum's success highlights Mohanlal's enduring box office appeal and versatility, with potential to reach ₹90 crore, also achieving over $1 million in North America.

In a fitting example of a superstar outshining his own legacy, Mohanlal’s latest film Thudarum has officially become the sixth highest-grossing Malayalam film of all time, surpassing the lifetime collections of his 2016 blockbuster Pulimurugan. The emotional family drama has so far amassed Rs 79.85 crore at the box office as per Sacnilk, edging past Pulimurugan’s Rs 76.67 crore haul.Directed by Tharun Moorthy, Thudarum presents Mohanlal in a soul-stirring drama.

As a movie buff and someone who loves to dive deep into the world of cinema, Thudarum felt wholesome and completely satisfied the movie fanatic in me.

The film grips you with a tension so real, you won’t dare blink. But what makes Thudarum stand apart is how it subtly, yet powerfully, reflects Mohanlal’s persona through symbolic storytelling.
